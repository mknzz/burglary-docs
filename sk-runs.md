# sk-runs

sk-runs is an illegal delivery job framework for QBCore and QBox servers. It is designed around tier-based jobs, multiple pickup and delivery scenarios, configurable reward systems, police pressure, and a public framework layer for adapting integrations.

## Highlights

- expandable tier system
- meetPed and meetVehicle scenario support
- reputation and leveling progression
- carry-prop visuals with network sync
- trunk attachment support for drop-off vehicles
- dirty money cleaning and optional phone-based job offers
- cooldowns, police requirements, and dispatch integration
